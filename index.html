The Problem
The Promise of DeFi
is Broken

Today's "decentralized" exchanges are centralized, unscalable, and carry existential regulatory risk.

Regulatory Risk & Centralization

Any utility token that appreciates with platform growth is deemed a security under the Howey Test. Governance tokens are not decentralization — they are centralized control with extra steps, creating a massive regulatory target.

Inherent Scaling Limits

Any account-based system is limited in throughput by design. Serialized transaction processing creates a bottleneck that prevents it from ever becoming a global settlement layer.

Poor Market Maker Environment

On-chain latency and unreliable transaction signals force market makers to widen spreads and carry excess capital, leading to thin, fragile liquidity.

The market has optimized for speculation, not fundamental infrastructure. The result is a fragmented ecosystem that cannot support global finance.

The Market Maker's Edge
Why UTXO Wins

Market making is a latency and risk management business. UTXO provides an information advantage that is structurally impossible on account-based systems.

▮ DXS — UTXO Model

Core Logic
Transactions reference explicit, unique inputs. Intent is unambiguous from the moment of propagation.
Signal Quality
Actionable Signal. Conflicting spends are immediately visible. Intent cannot be silently replaced.
Hedging
Immediate. Hedge execution can occur as soon as the transaction is seen on the network.
Spread
Tighter — lower risk allows tighter spreads
Capital
Higher efficiency — less held in reserve
Execution
<20ms target
vs

▮ Others — Account-Based

Core Logic
Transactions mutate shared account state. Multiple transactions compete to modify the same nonce.
Signal Quality
Provisional Intent. Until block inclusion, ordering is uncertain and transactions can be replaced or repriced.
Hedging
Delayed. Must wait for a higher degree of certainty, increasing unhedged exposure and inventory risk.
Spread
Wider — uncertainty and risk require wider spreads
Capital
Lower efficiency — more capital buffering uncertainty
Execution
~50ms (Hyperliquid) to seconds (Ethereum)
In on-chain order books, UTXO turns network propagation into a trading signal. Account-based models turn it into a question mark.
